# Code of Conduct

Bridge CLI has adopted the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its code of conduct.

All contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to follow it.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **hello@bridge.ls**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
